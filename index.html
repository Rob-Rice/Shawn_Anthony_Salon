About Shawn Anthony Salon

Shawn Anthony Salon Spa is located in the heart of downtown Homewood. Our spa is nestled among the areas most popular boutiques and eateries. We have been in business 16 years and have been in this location for 10. We offer tailored solutions for our guests hair and skin. We specialize in hair coloring, balayage, traditional hilites, women’s cutting and styling as well as men’s cuts and styling based on lifestyle, profession, and individual aesthetics.
